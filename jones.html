Bio

Jonathan Dwight Jones[1] (born July 19, 1987)[2] is an American professional mixed martial artist. He currently competes in the Heavyweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), where he is the current UFC Heavyweight Champion.[2] A professional since 2008, Jones was previously the UFC Light Heavyweight Champion from 2011 to 2015 and from 2018 to 2020, as well as the interim UFC Light Heavyweight Champion in 2016.[2] As of November 19, 2024, he is #2 in the UFC men's pound-for-pound rankings.[19] He is regarded as one of the greatest mixed martial artists of all time.[20]
